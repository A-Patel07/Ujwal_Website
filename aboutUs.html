Our Misson

At UjwalUSA, we aim to help those in rural Zambia. This all started a few years ago when we traveled to Africa. On our trip, we noticed families living in rural villages faced several challenge. Among these include transportation, environmental, education, and vision problems. And so, UjwalUSA was created to assist those who could use it. We are based in central New Jersey and would be grateful for anyone willing to donate.
